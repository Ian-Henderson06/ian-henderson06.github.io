Tower Duels

Unity - C#

Tower Duels is a strategic versus tower defence game. The aim of the game is to destroy your opponents base tile (shown at the bottom of their side of the screen) by spending money to send troops to their side of the board, whilst attempting to defend your own side by purchasing and placing turrets.

I created this game with my friend as part of the Duck Sauce Game Jam 2023. I created the various interaction systems used within the game. Turret interactions and shooting as well as designing most of the UI, colours and overall theme of the game.
